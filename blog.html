It is beautiful even to exist

Life, a small four lettered word with a big meaning. From childhood the constant noise of make our lives better has been echoing in our ears.
But what is life ? Life is the art of existing, and the best thing about art is that everyone has his/her own taste. Every art is beautiful, even if there is no art and just existence. Some admire the art of others, some improve their own and some paint it slowly and carefully. The beauty of this art is the subjectivity. We all start with a canvas, some with a beautiful outline on it, some with a scribbled canvas. But as you know, it is the artist who matters, the artist can take inspiration from others, but the final work is his, it has its own originality, its own perfections and its own flaws and no mortal in this world has the right to judge it. We all are artists perfect in our own ways, some leave their canvas imcomplete, some don't get the time to complete their canvas, some have a difficult time in painting their canvas, some lack the colors to paint their, some share their colors with others, some like their art black and white, some like it colorful, but at the end we all are artist trying to complete and perfect their arts. So stay, stay, your art is incomplete, your art needs you. The universe is meant just to be seen by your eyes, how rare and beautiful it is even to exist. Everything is here, just see one more time and with shortness of breath explain the infinite.

Shaunak Kaushik
